Protect the Hotsprings. Safeguard Our Water. Empower the Community.

The Kariandusi WRUA stewards the Kariandusi Hotsprings and the greater Lake Elementaita catchment—restoring wetlands, improving water quality, and building resilient livelihoods through eco‑tourism and community water access.

Give Now See Our Projects

Our Mission

To conserve the Kariandusi Hotsprings ecosystem, protect the Lake Elementaita UNESCO site, and expand safe, reliable water supply for surrounding villages—through science‑based catchment management, local stewardship, and nature‑positive tourism.

  • Water quality monitoring & watershed restoration
  • Community water points and pipeline upgrades
  • Eco‑tourism trails, guides, and visitor facilities
  • Youth jobs & conservation training

Current Priority Projects

Water Quality & Wetland Restoration

Quarterly sampling, spring protection, erosion control, and native re‑vegetation to reduce sedimentation into the lake.

Community Water Access

Upgrading pipelines, kiosks, and storage—bringing clean, reliable water closer to homes and schools.

Eco‑Tourism Hub

Low‑impact trails, guide training, and visitor amenities to generate sustainable income for conservation.
